How It Works

01
Reference basket
LAMP tracks 17 political event contracts across Polymarket and Kalshi, weighted by liquidity depth and open interest. Rebalanced monthly.
02
Total return swap
Institutional swap counterparties provide synthetic exposure — no on-chain custody or crypto wallet required by investors.
03
Contracts resolve
Each contract pays $1 (YES) or $0 (NO) on resolution. Triple-source oracle: AP, Fox News, NBC for elections. Fed.gov for macro.
04
NAV updates
Fund NAV marks daily from composite mid prices. Resolved positions replaced at the next monthly rebalance window.
05
Quarterly liquidity
Redemptions available quarterly with 30-day notice. Matches the real liquidity profile of the underlying prediction market contracts.
